Chicken Biryani


Chicken biryani is a delicious Pakistani/Indian rice dish that's typically reserved for special occasions such as weddings, parties, or holidays such as Ramadan. It has a lengthy preparation, but the work is definitely worth it. For biryani, basmati rice is the ideal variety to use.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided
  • Onions and Tomatoes
  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Whole Spices
  • Chilli Powder, Salt, Biryani Masala
  • Yogurt
  • Chicken
  • Basmati Rice

Steps

  1. Soak Basmati Rice and keep it aside
  2. Add Whole spices, Onions, Tomatoes, Oil and Ginger Garlic and fry them
  3. Add Marinated chicken, mix and wait till chicken is cooked
  4. Add Soaked Basmati Rice and required water to cook
  5. Serve it with Raita and Enjoy!
